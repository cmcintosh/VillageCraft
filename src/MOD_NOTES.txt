
Village Craft notes:

Village Stats:
  Population - Calculates based on the number of registered villagers
  Honor - Based on Good/Bad deeds performed by the villagers towards other villages
  Wealth - Calculated based on the goods that are stored in one of the village warehouse chests.
  Strength - Based on the number of soldier villagers + lvl of those mods + equipment
  Border - provides the radius for the border of the village
  InteractionZone - provides the radius for the interaction area for villagers with other villages
  Level - Provides the level of the village.
  



1. Village Centers - identify the central spot of the village, and allows villagers to register as members of the village. 
   the borders of the village are calculated from the center of the village.
